Travel Insurance Dashboard
An interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze travel insurance sales, commission performance, customer demographics, claims distribution, and top travel destinations. The dashboard provides actionable insights that support business decision-making and performance monitoring within the travel insurance industry.

## Key Features
- Sales & commission analysis by agency type
- Claims distribution by gender and product category
- Customer age-group analysis
- Top travel destinations by net sales
- Online vs offline channel performance comparison
- Interactive filters and KPI tracking

## KPIs Included
- Total Net Sales: $2.58M
- Total Commission: $621.23K
- Average Commission: $22.1
- Commission Rate: 24.10
- Transaction Volume: 63K
- Claim Rate: 1.46

## Business Impact
This dashboard helps identify sales trends, customer behavior, profitable distribution channels, and claim patterns, enabling data-driven decision-making and improved operational performance.
